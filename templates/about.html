About the Project

Ethical AI Response Evaluator reviews AI-generated content and provides a lightweight ethical score focused on safety, bias, privacy, toxicity, and misinformation risk.

How the System Works

The system takes a prompt or response, generates or collects the model output, runs rule-based evaluation checks, summarizes risky patterns, and presents the final ethical score with explanation and issue highlighting.

How to Use the System

  1. Enter AI response or prompt text on the home dashboard.
  2. Click Evaluate.
  3. Review the ethical score, explanation, chart, and highlighted issues.

Ethical AI Importance

Responsible AI matters because generated content can amplify bias, expose private information, encourage unsafe actions, or spread misinformation. Fast evaluation helps teams catch these problems early before deployment or submission.
